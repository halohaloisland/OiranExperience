The Artisans 職人の技

Decades of Dedication to Beauty.

Our experience is brought to you by a veteran hairdresser couple with over 40 years of dedication to the art of Japanese styling. We believe in "Omotenashi" - wholehearted hospitality.

Every hairpin placed, every kimono fold adjusted, is done with the expertise that only a lifetime of practice can provide. We invite you to experience not just a costume change, but a transformation into the elegant history of Japan.

The Experience 体験内容

Immerse yourself in tradition.

Authentic Styling

Intricate "Date-hyogo" and other traditional styles created with real hair and premium extensions, adorned with golden Kanzashi.

Premium Kimono

Wear genuine, high-quality silk Kimonos. Our collection features vibrant colors and gold embroidery fit for an Oiran.

Photography

Capture your transformation with professional lighting setups or enjoy a self-guided photoshoot in our traditional studio sets.
